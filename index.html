Questions

Common questions.

What will my team actually build?
Real, working software. Internal tools, client-facing prototypes, dashboards, automations. In the Spark format, everyone builds a starter project. In the Intensive and Transformation formats, they build tools for actual business use cases your team identifies.
Does my team need technical experience?
No. These sessions are designed for non-technical teams. AI tools handle the technical complexity – your team focuses on what to build, not how to code it. I've run this with people who have never written a line of code.
How is this different from AI training we've already done?
Most AI training is presentations and prompt tips. This is hands-on building. Your team doesn't watch someone use AI – they build working software themselves and leave with deployed projects they can actually use.
Can this be done virtually?
Yes. In-person is ideal for the full-day and 4-week formats. Virtual works well for the 2-hour Spark format and for distributed teams.
What do participants need?
A laptop and a Claude Code subscription (~$20/month). I provide setup guides before the session and walk everyone through the tools on day one. No pre-work required.
